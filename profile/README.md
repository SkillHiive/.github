# SkillHiive

**We're building communities that value presence over performance.**

The internet has become incredibly good at capturing attention.

Algorithms optimize for engagement.
Feeds optimize for scrolling.
Platforms optimize for retention.

We're interested in something else.

We believe the best communities aren't built around metrics, followers, or endless notifications. They're built around trust, collaboration, and people choosing to show up for one another.

SkillHiive is an open-source platform exploring a different direction.

One where:

* Presence matters more than activity.
* Conversations stay between the people they're meant for.
* Communities grow through shared work—not endless content.
* Technology supports human connection instead of competing for attention.

We don't believe software should constantly demand your time.

We believe it should help you spend that time well.

## Why Open Source?

Because communities deserve software they can trust.

Open source gives people the ability to understand how the platform works, host it themselves, improve it, and build on top of it.

Transparency isn't a feature for us.

It's part of the foundation.

## Our Principles

### Presence

Technology should create space for meaningful interaction—not constant interruption.

### Privacy

Not every conversation needs an audience.

Private spaces should remain private.

### Intention

People should decide when they engage.

Software shouldn't manipulate them into coming back.

### Allies

Communities aren't audiences.

They're groups of people building something together.

## Building in Public

SkillHiive is still evolving.

Every release, discussion, bug report, feature request, and contribution helps shape where it goes next.

If our philosophy resonates with you, we'd love for you to be part of that journey.
